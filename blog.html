Blissful Benefits

Blissful Benefits is Southeast Airlines' frequent flyers program. We offer three levels-- Supreme, Superior, and Superb.

Supreme

Being a Supreme member is the best way to fly. Supreme members are the first passengers to board Southeast Airplanes, and are the first members on the plane to receive complimentary food and beverages. Supreme members can obtain assistance at any time during the flight. In addition, Supreme passengers are offered a reclining chair that extends to a bed. Supreme members can sleep peacefully and comfortably on every Southeast flight. They receive an emergency kit containing a faux fur eye mask, a toothbrush, toothpaste, mouthwash, and chapstick. They are also given a pillow, two complimentary cashmere blankets and a pair of wool fuzzy slippers. Supreme members are also offered complimentary champagne for passengers over the age of 21 during Southeastern flights. They also have a separate waiting line than Superior and Superb members in all airports for Southeast flights. This cuts their waiting time in half. Supreme members are also the first passengers to exit the plane. That way, Supreme members do not have to stress about missing their next flight.

Superior

Superior members have the second best status. Superior members are the second in line to board Southeast Airplanes. On the flight, Superior members are given a mini-emergency kit containing an eye mask, a mini toothbrush, and toothpaste. Superior members are also offered a complimentary glass of champagne for passengers over the age of 21. Superior members have a separate waiting line from Superb and Supreme members. Additionally, Superior members are given a comfortable reclining chair, and are given a pillow, a cashmere blanket, and a pair of wool fuzzy slippers. Superior members are the second in line to exit the plane. Superior members are also offered assistance at any time on the flight.

Superb

Superb members receive a comfortable chair with a pillow, a blanket and a pair of cotton slippers. They receive a mini-emergency kit containing a mini-toothbrush with mini-toothpaste. Superb members are the last to board and exit Southeast airlines. Superb members are also offered assistance at any time on the flight.
